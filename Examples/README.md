# Joint rotations

Rotations in ~~strikethrough~~ do not typically occur in the human body or wheelchair

## Body

| Joint         | + Bend                           | - Bend                          | + Tilt                                          | - Tilt                                   | + Twist                                  | - Twist                                   |
|---------------|----------------------------------|---------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| Head, Neck    | Flexion (tilting down)           | Extension (tilting up)          | Left lateral flexion (tilting left)             | Right lateral flexion (tilting right)    | Right rotation (turning right)           | Left rotation (turning left)              |
| Wrist         | Flexion (lifting palm up)        | Extension (lowering palm down)  | Ulnar deviation (tilting towards little finger) | Radial deviation (tilting towards thumb) | ~~Pronation (rotating palm down)~~       | ~~Supination (rotating palm up)~~         |
| Elbow         | Flexion (bending)                | Extension (straightening)       | ~~Abduction (away from body midline)~~          | ~~Adduction (towards body midline)~~     | Pronation (rotating palm down)           | Supination (rotating palm up)             |
| Shoulder      | Abduction (raising arm sideways) | Adduction (lowering arm)        | Internal rotation (twisting inward)             | External rotation (twisting outward)     | Flexion (raising arm forward)            | Extension (lowering arm)                  |
| Clavicle      | Elevation (raising shoulders)    | Depression (lowering shoulders) | ~~Internal rotation (twisting inward)~~         | ~~External rotation (twisting outward)~~ | Protraction (pushing shoulders forward)  | Retraction (pulling shoulders back)       |
| Torso, Lumbar | Flexion (bending forwards)       | Extension (arching backwards)   | Left lateral flexion (leaning left)             | Right lateral flexion (leaning right)    | Right rotation (turning right)           | Left rotation (turning left)              |
| Toe           | Flexion (curling)                | Extension (straightening)       | ~~Abduction (toes separating)~~                 | ~~Adduction (toes coming together)~~     | ~~Internal rotation (twisting inwards)~~ | ~~External rotation (twisting outwards)~~ |
| Ankle         | Dorsiflexion (lifting foot)      | Plantarflexion (lowering foot)  | Eversion (rolling out)                          | Inversion (rolling in)                   | ~~Internal rotation (turning inwards)~~  | ~~External rotation (turning outwards)~~  |
| Knee          | Flexion (bending)                | Extension (straightening)       | ~~Abduction (spreading apart)~~                 | ~~Adduction (coming together)~~          | ~~Internal rotation (turning inwards)~~  | ~~External rotation (turning outwards)~~  |
| Hip           | Flexion (lifting leg forwards)   | Extension (lowering leg)        | Abduction (lifting leg sideways)                | Adduction (lowering leg)                 | Internal rotation (turning inwards)      | External rotation (turning outwards)      |
| Pelvis        | Leaning forwards                 | Leaning backwards               | Leaning left                                    | Leaning right                            | Turning left                             | Turning right                             |
